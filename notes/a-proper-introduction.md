# A Proper Introduction

I am, at the time of writing this, an eighteen year old in the gap between school and college. I have several interests; but I haven't pursued any of them deeply enough. This personal blog is an attempt to change that.

My interests approximately revolve around the intersection of the arts and the sciences. The Dionysian and the Apollonian.

What can you expect here? Honestly, I don't know myself. Perhaps minor ramblings, some essays. Maybe book notes? Hopefully by the time an actual visitor stumbles upon this page, there is something to read. 

What's the deal with semicolons? WELL, I liked em dashes. I used them everywhere. I remember a teacher pointing out excessive em dashes in a school essay. But then, AI took over them. I like semicolons now. Partly a rebellion, partly an aesthetic. 

Hopefully this was a sufficient introduction. I'll update this post with time.
